Overview

ACE Sector 12 Noida Extension is a luxury residential project developed by the renowned ACE Group in Greater Noida West. The project offers spacious 3 and 4 BHK apartments, designed to provide a modern and comfortable lifestyle. These apartments feature contemporary interiors with high-end finishes, ensuring ample natural light, ventilation, and space optimization. With a focus on luxury, the project includes a variety of amenities such as a well-equipped clubhouse, a large swimming pool, fitness center, and sports facilities like tennis and badminton courts. For families, there are dedicated children’s play areas, all set amidst landscaped gardens and ample green spaces, promoting a healthy and eco-friendly living environment.
